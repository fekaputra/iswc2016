There are three hotels in the walking distance.

Kobe Portpia Hotel

Kobe Portpia Hotel, which is connected to the venue, is 4 star hotel and provides various rooms from executive suite rooms to single rooms. The room price ranges from 100 USD to 1,500 USD.

Hotel website
(English included)

Ariston Hotel Kobe

Ariston Hotel Kobe, which is adjunct to the venue, is 3 star hotel and offers single, double, and twin rooms with ca. 100 USD per room. Hotel Pearl City Kobe is also 3 star hotel and offers single, double, and twin rooms from 60 USD.

Hotel website
(Japanese only)

Another choice is staying in the city area which can reach from the venue in 10 minutes by the automated guided transit. There are many hotels ranging 4-star hotels to hostels good for students.
