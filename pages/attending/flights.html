It is convenient to use Kansai Int’l Airport (KIX) to reach the venue. There are some direct flights from/to Europe, Middle-east, and North America. The simplest way is to use these flights. The other way is to reach there via Tokyo/Narita Int’l Airport (NRT). There are a wide variety of flights between Narita (NRT) and major European cities and there are connecting flights between NRT and Kansai Int’l Airport (KIX) by Japan Airlines (OneWorld) and All Nippon Airways (Star Alliance).

There are many direct flights between KIX and major cities in Asia.

The following is information about direct flights and some excerpts for connecting flights. They are based on the current flight schedule subject to change.

Note: MTWTFSS means the available days of a flight that are Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday respectively, but a missing day - has no flight.
