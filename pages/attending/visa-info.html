Local Restrictions

Japan is open to many countries. 66 countries and regions are visa exemptions. Please see the page in detail. http://www.mofa.go.jp/j_info/visit/visa/short/novisa.html There are no restrictions for foreigners to travel all over Japan.

The currency for the local conference accounting is Yen (JPY). 1 USD is 100-120 JPY approximately. There are no major restrictions to have international conferences for the conference venue.
