Biography

Christian Bizer is a Professor at the Univesity of Mannheim, where is also Chair of Web-based Systems and Director of the Institute of Computer Science and Business Informatics. He is well known in the community for his work on DBpedia, Linked Open Data, and microdata/microformats.
