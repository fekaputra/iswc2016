We are pleased to announce three levels of sponsorship: Silver, Gold and Platinum.

Custom-tailored sponsorships to better suit your needs can also be agreed on demand. Contact the sponsorship chairs for further details

Platinum ($7500)

  • Acknowledgement of your support in our proceedings
